# Gameplay Loop

Primary screen flow outline:

1. BootScreen
2. MainMenuScreen
3. FranchiseHubScreen
4. Roster and management flows (Roster, Lineup, Rotation, Schedule, Standings, Inbox, Scouting)
5. GameDayScreen
6. LiveMatchScreen
7. BoxScoreScreen
8. PostGameScreen

LiveMatchScreen is the centerpiece and owns field view, runner movement, pitch outcomes, overlays, and pause/manager commands.
